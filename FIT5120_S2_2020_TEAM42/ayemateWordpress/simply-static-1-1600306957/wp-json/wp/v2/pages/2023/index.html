There\u2019s a lot of focus on what a nutritious diet can do for children\u2019s physical health, like proper growth, stronger bones, and even a lower risk for chronic disease in adulthood. But what about their mental health?\u00a0Improving mental health might initially seem like it has nothing to do with what we eat but there is a strong correlation between what we eat and how we feel socially and emotionally!<\/p>

Poor diet can make your child feel low, lethargic and might increase the symptoms of depression and anxiety as well. On the other hand, having a nutritious diet helps you keep a healthy heart and weight that in turn reduces the risk of chronic diseases. It also helps with sleeping patterns. Human brain needs an adequate amount of nutrients to stay active.<\/p>

The type of food we eat has the ability to affect our mood. So choose your diet wisely so as to lift your children\u2019s mood, energy levels and concentration.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
