We think there is increasing demand of online mental health such as telepsychology and telepsychiatry especially for those living in rural, regional or interstate areas and in other situations where face-to-face consultations are impracticable.<\/span><\/p>\n

Telehealth is the delivery of health care at a distance using information and communications technology. Telehealth enables consumers to have quality health care from their comfort of their home, work or anywhere<\/p>\n

Using Tele services will improve the accessibility to healthcare for our Gippsland’s community. <\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t

Benefits of telepsychiatry consultations<\/strong><\/h4>

Video-based telepsychiatry helps meet patients\u2019 needs for convenient, affordable and readily-accessible mental health services. It can benefit patients in a number of ways, such as:<\/span>
<\/strong><\/p>

  • Greater access to medical care after hours<\/li>
  • Improve access to mental health specialty care that might not otherwise be available (e.g., in rural areas)<\/li>
  • Bulk billed<\/li>
  • Bring care to the patient\u2019s location (no travel costs, out of pocket consultation costs, or waiting room times)<\/li>
  • Access to a wide variety of sub-specialists<\/li>
  • Prompt appointments and feedback to referring doctors<\/li>
  • Easy referral process<\/li>
  • Reduce delays in care<\/li>
  • Reduce potential transportation barriers, such as lack of transportation or the need for long drives<\/li><\/ul><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
