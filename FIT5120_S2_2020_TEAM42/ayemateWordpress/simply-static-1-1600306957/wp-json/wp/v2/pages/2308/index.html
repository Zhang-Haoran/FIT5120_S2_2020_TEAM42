Children with anxiety dif\ufb01culties tend to lack con\ufb01dence in their abilities and feel overwhelmed easily. They are also driven to avoid the things that cause them anxiety, and in doing so, don\u2019t get the chance to learn that what they fear will usually not happen. You can help your children by working on coping and problem-solving skills together through the help of mental health programs offered by several  organisations. <\/p>\n

Good mental health is critical to the wellbeing and development of every child. Helping build resilience and positive self-esteem, good mental health will enable your child to build respectful relationships as they grow up.<\/p>\n

Wellbeing programs will help your child to:<\/b><\/p>\n

    \n
  • Feel happy and positive about themselves and enjoy life <\/span><\/li>\n
  • Have healthy relationships with family and friends <\/span><\/li>\n
  • Physically active and eat a healthy diet <\/span><\/li>\n
  • Involved in a range of activities <\/span><\/li>\n
  • Have a sense of achievement <\/span><\/li>\n
  • Can relax and have a good night\u2019s sleep <\/span><\/li>\n
  • Feel like they belong to their community <\/span><\/li>\n<\/ul><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
