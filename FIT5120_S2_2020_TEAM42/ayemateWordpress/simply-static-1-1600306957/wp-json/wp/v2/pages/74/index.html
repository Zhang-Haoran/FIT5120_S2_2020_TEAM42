Anxiety is a common phenomenon where people react to something they fear, worry or stress about. However, children react more quickly or intensely to situations they find threatening, or find it harder to get their anxious feelings under control.<\/p>


\u00a0A child’s mental health is influenced by many things; family circumstances, friendship groups and school and life experience. While children of any age can experience mental health challenges, they are most at risk between 12 and 16 years.\u00a0<\/p>


Mental health problems at young ages can develop into a disorder and can have a substantial impact on wellbeing. Helping build resilience and positive self-esteem, good mental health will enable your child to build respectful relationships as they grow up.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
