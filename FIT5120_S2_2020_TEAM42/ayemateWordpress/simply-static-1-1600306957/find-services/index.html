Health services

Getting right help at the right time is very crucial and doctor is often a good place to start for most mental health issues, as they can make a diagnosis, provide treatment or refer you to other mental health services if needed.

Visiting your local GP to discuss a mental health issue has many benefits as they are able to provide care for both physical and mental health issues.

Before Going to Hospital 

 Be prepared for the visit that will help the doctor to understand your child’s situation effectively.

  • Have your reasons behind the visist ready and make a list of the key points you would like to discuss andany questions you would like to ask.
  • Find out what you can about your family’s medical history as this can help your doctor to identify the health issue you are experiencing.
  • If you have not seen this particular doctor before carry any medications your child is currently using/taking.
